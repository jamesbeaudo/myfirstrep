How to use the data?

Transparency is a great tool to truly understand the ins-and-outs of your industry; at the condition of respecting employee privacy. It's why we provide an anonymous way to users to express themselves, and learn more about their surroundings. Here are three suggestions on how to maximize team learning:

  1. Send an annual email giving an overview of how the people making up this organization like to work, alongside resources for self-development.
  2. Use it as constructive criticism, or a way to encourage self-development. Ask the parties involved to come up with a personal goal to achieve.
  3. Use it as a benchmark for future reference; alongside employee satisfaction, it can help you, as a whole organization, keep track of everyone, and making sure their voice is heard.
